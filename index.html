It's A Doggie Life

Let's face it... Some of us can't go anywhere without our little canine counterparts! Well, I guess you are in luck! You've stumbled upon the right webpage here. Across our beautiful country there are many dog friendly restaurants for you and your buddy to chose from. Here is a list of places you can plan to visit for that special meal.

Loko

Loko is a award winning restrsint situated in Waterford. They have a amazinng and diverse menu. From beautiful pastries in the mornings, amazing mixed platers in the evening and little treats for your furry friendly. Owners Jamie and Nicola Dowling share a beautiful love for animals and are also active in local animal services.

360 Cookhouse

The 360 Cookhyouse is located in Dungarven Co.Waterford. It has a beautful decor and sesign to the building. The food is beautiful and offers fresh fish, steaks and stone baked pizzas. They also have a canine menu with items such as doggie meat bones and pigs ear! They were voted - The Irish Times Top 100 restaurants for lunch.

Visit 360 Cookhouse On Facebook

The Doghouse Blues Tearoom & Restaurant

The Doghouse Blues Tearoom & Restaurant is located by Howth Dart Station, Co.Dublin. It is one of Dublins latest opening restraunts which dosent shut itsd doors until 11 o'clock. They have a great selection of a A La Carte menu, A cosy decor with a snug fire place to make you and your dog feel right at home.
