It's A Doggie Life

Let's face it... Some of us can't go anywhere without our little canine counterparts! Well, I guess you are in luck! You've stumbled upon the right web page here. Across our beautiful country there are many dog friendly restaurants for you and your buddy to chose from. Here is a list of places you can plan to visit for that special meal.

Loko

Loko is an award-winning restaurant situated in Co. Waterford. They have an amazing and diverse menu. From beautiful pastries in the mornings, amazing mixed platters in the evening and little treats for your furry friendly. Owners Jamie and Nicola Dowling share a beautiful love for animals and are also active in local animal services.

Visit Loko On Facebook.

360 Cookhouse

The 360 Cookhouse is located in Dungarven Co. Waterford. It has a dazzling decor and design to the building. The food is beautiful and offers fresh fish, steaks and stone baked pizzas. They also have a canine menu with items such as doggie meat bones and pigs ear! They were voted - The Irish Times Top 100 restaurants for lunch.

Visit 360 Cookhouse On Facebook.

The Doghouse Blues Tearoom & Restaurant
